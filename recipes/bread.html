Garlic Bread Recipe

Recipe Description

For this classic garlic bread, start by preheating your oven to 180°C (350°F). Slice a baguette or loaf of Italian bread in half lengthwise, then place the halves on a baking sheet, cut side up.

Next, make the garlic butter by mixing softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley in a small bowl. Spread this mixture generously on each bread half, covering the surface evenly. This will ensure each bite has a rich garlic flavor.

Once ready to bake, place the bread in the oven for about 10–15 minutes or until it’s golden and crispy. For a little extra crisp, you can broil it for a minute or two at the end. Serve warm as a side dish or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette or Italian bread loaf, halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup softened butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Slice the bread in half lengthwise and place on a baking sheet, cut side up.
  3. Mix softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley in a bowl.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly on each bread half.
  5. Bake for 10–15 minutes or until golden and crispy, optionally broiling for extra crunch.
  6. Serve warm as a side or appetizer.
