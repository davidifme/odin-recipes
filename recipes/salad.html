Caprese Salad Recipe

Recipe Description

This refreshing Caprese Salad is perfect as an appetizer or side dish. Start by slicing fresh tomatoes and mozzarella into even, round pieces. Arrange them in alternating layers on a serving plate, adding fresh basil leaves between each layer. This classic Italian dish is as beautiful as it is tasty.

Next, season the salad with a pinch of salt and black pepper to taste. Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the entire plate, making sure each piece is coated lightly. For an added depth of flavor, sprinkle a little balsamic vinegar over the salad or use a balsamic reduction.

Finish with a garnish of extra basil leaves, if desired. This salad is best served immediately to enjoy the fresh, vibrant flavors. Caprese Salad makes a great light starter or can be enjoyed on its own as a refreshing, healthy snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 250g fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • Balsamic vinegar or reduction (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella into even rounds.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices in alternating layers on a serving plate, with fresh basil leaves between each slice.
  3. Season the salad with salt and black pepper.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salad, adding balsamic vinegar or reduction if desired.
  5. Garnish with extra basil leaves and serve immediately.
